About Me

Hi! My name is Emily and I am a piano player from Washington. I love playing the piano and it is an absolute joy to share it with all of you!

My Goal


My goal and hope is that you too may find your passion and love in music regardless of genre or instrument. I personally believe that everyone should learn an instrument in their lifetime, regardless of age, gender, or skill.
